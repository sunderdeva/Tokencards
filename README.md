# TokenCards

## Retail gift cards for tokenized stocks & crypto

TokenCards is a retail-based financial onboarding platform that enables users to purchase physical gift cards in kiosks, trains and stores, and redeem them for cryptocurrencies and tokenized stocks through a simple QR-based flow.

## Problem
Millions of people remain excluded from traditional financial markets due to banking barriers, complexity, and lack of access to digital platforms.

## Solution
TokenCards bridges physical retail and digital finance by offering prepaid gift cards that can be redeemed for crypto assets and tokenized equities, lowering friction and enabling mass adoption.

## How it works
1. User buys a TokenCard in a physical retail location
2. Activates code
3. Connect with Chatterpay Wallet
4. Redeems balance into crypto or tokenized stocks

## Key Features
- Physical retail distribution
- Crypto & tokenized equity support
- Non-custodial wallet integration
- KYC-light / tiered compliance
- Instant redemption
- Educational onboarding

## Business Model
- Fee per card activation
- Conversion/spread fee
- Revenue sharing with issuers
- White-label for retailers

## Tech Stack (proposed)
- Smart contracts (ERC-20 / ERC-3643)
- QR-based redemption system
- Wallet integrations
- Compliance layer (tiered KYC)
- Backend settlement engine

## Roadmap

- Retail partnerships
- LatAm expansion
- Tokenized assets expansion

## Vision
Make financial markets accessible to everyone, starting from the corner kiosk.
